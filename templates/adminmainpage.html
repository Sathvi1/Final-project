What is Block Chain Certification.

Blockchain-based certificate generation and validation leverages the decentralized and secure nature of blockchain technology to create tamper-proof and easily verifiable digital certificates, addressing the challenges of traditional certificate management.

Here's a breakdown of the process:


Digital Certificate Creation:

Certificates are generated and stored digitally, often using smart contracts on a blockchain network.

Hashing and Blockchain Storage:

Each certificate's information (including digital signature) is converted into a unique hash and stored as a transaction on the blockchain.
